Acrocordia megalospora (Fink) R. C. Harris
Minnesota, Yellow Medicine Co: ca 5 mi S, 4.5 mi E Granite Falls. Upper Sioux Agency State Park. T115N R38W section 29 NE1/4 of SW1/4. 44.7350°N, 95.4567°W (determined by GPS; datum = WGS 84). Elevation 1010 ft.
Open Quercus macrocarpa forest and grassland transition on steep, NE-facing slopes above Minnesota River. Scattered granite boulders.
